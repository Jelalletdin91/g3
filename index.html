About us

Global Group has been providing expert consultancy services in education and international employment across Turkey and Europe since 2018. Since our establishment, we have helped thousands of individuals pursue their dream education and build successful careers abroad. Our goal is to empower students, young professionals, and job seekers to discover their potential and guide them on the right path toward their goals. With a broad network of institutions, trusted partners, and an experienced team of consultants, we offer personalized and strategic solutions tailored to each individual. At Global Group, we are more than just a consultancy firm — we aim to be your partner on this journey. From education planning to job placement, we support you every step of the way with transparency, trust, and professionalism.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading force in the sector across Turkey and Europe by helping individuals become globally competent, informed, and confident.

Our Mission

Our mission is to guide our clients in making the right decisions and walking confidently toward a brighter future.
